Thanks for comming to this place…

What is this website?

For now this blog is my [locus communis](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/locus_communis), an open book for ideas. This is also a journal about my path into technology and philosophy. Maybe you agree with them, maybe not. Just try to be constructive in your opinions, without using pejorative language.

Please take into consideration that English is my second language and maybe you will find the construction of some sentences a little bit odd.

…and why are you charing this?

I want to find people with a common interest and share opinions about the subjects mentioned here. A second reason is to leave a piece on my mind here, as an echo of who I am and what I thought.

Also, sometimes I found useful knowledge on the Internet that I want to share in a blog format, following the [KISS principle!](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KISS_principle). I’ll try to keep it short too, 5 to 10 minutes reading per post with some exceptions from time to time.

Who Am I?

I was a university professor for 10 years, teaching Calculus, Geometry, Topology, Computer Graphics, Digital Image Processing, and Modern Algebra.

After those years, I switched to work in the private sector I have been working there for over a decade. I have used many hats (front developer, back developer, web developer, machine learning engineer, data engineer, data architect, and roles as a leader (Scrum Master, Data & Analytics Lead, Chief Data Architect, Data and Analytics Manager, etc).

But, in the end, I didn’t find myself in the corporate world so now, to be honest, I want to focus exclusively on those things that I like the most and share my experience with others. I hope you find the contents of this blog useful in some way.

That’s all… thanks and I’ll see you when I see you…
